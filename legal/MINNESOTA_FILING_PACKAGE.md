## FORM 2: CORPORATE BYLAWS (Template)

**BYLAWS**
**OF**
**BLACKROAD CORPORATION**
**A Minnesota Business Corporation**

---

### ARTICLE I - OFFICES

**Section 1.1 Principal Office.** The principal office of the corporation shall be located at such place as may be determined by the Board of Directors.

**Section 1.2 Registered Office.** The registered office required by law shall be maintained at the address stated in the Articles of Incorporation or as subsequently designated by the Board.

### ARTICLE II - SHAREHOLDERS

**Section 2.1 Annual Meeting.** An annual meeting of shareholders shall be held at such time and place as determined by the Board of Directors for the purpose of electing directors and transacting other business.

**Section 2.2 Special Meetings.** Special meetings may be called by the Board of Directors, the President, or shareholders holding at least 10% of voting shares.

**Section 2.3 Notice.** Written notice of meetings shall be given not less than 10 nor more than 60 days before the meeting date.

**Section 2.4 Quorum.** A majority of the outstanding shares entitled to vote, represented in person or by proxy, shall constitute a quorum.

**Section 2.5 Voting.** Each share of common stock shall be entitled to one vote. Cumulative voting is not permitted.

### ARTICLE III - BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**Section 3.1 Number.** The Board shall consist of one (1) or more directors as set by the shareholders. Initially: one (1) director.

**Section 3.2 Term.** Directors shall be elected at the annual meeting and serve one-year terms.

**Section 3.3 Powers.** The Board shall manage the business and affairs of the corporation.

**Section 3.4 Meetings.** The Board may meet at such times and places as determined by resolution.

**Section 3.5 Quorum.** A majority of directors shall constitute a quorum.

**Section 3.6 Compensation.** Directors may receive reasonable compensation as determined by the Board.

### ARTICLE IV - OFFICERS

**Section 4.1 Officers.** The officers of the corporation shall include:
- President (Chief Executive Officer)
- Secretary
- Treasurer (Chief Financial Officer)
- Such other officers as the Board may appoint

**Section 4.2 Election and Term.** Officers shall be elected by the Board and serve at the pleasure of the Board.

**Section 4.3 President.** The President shall:
- Serve as Chief Executive Officer
- Preside at shareholder and Board meetings
- Execute contracts and documents on behalf of the corporation
- Supervise day-to-day operations

**Section 4.4 Secretary.** The Secretary shall:
- Keep minutes of meetings
- Maintain corporate records
- Give notice of meetings as required

**Section 4.5 Treasurer.** The Treasurer shall:
- Oversee financial management
- Keep accurate financial records
- Prepare financial reports

### ARTICLE V - STOCK

**Section 5.1 Certificates.** Shares may be certificated or uncertificated as determined by the Board.

**Section 5.2 Transfer.** Shares shall be transferable on the books of the corporation, subject to applicable securities laws.

**Section 5.3 Lost Certificates.** The corporation may issue replacement certificates upon satisfactory proof of loss and receipt of an indemnity bond.

### ARTICLE VI - FISCAL YEAR

The fiscal year of the corporation shall be determined by the Board of Directors. Initially: January 1 to December 31.

### ARTICLE VII - AMENDMENTS

These Bylaws may be amended by a majority vote of the Board of Directors or by a majority vote of the shareholders.

## FORM 6: MINNESOTA TAX REGISTRATION

### Minnesota Department of Revenue Registration

**Steps:**

1. **Register for Minnesota Tax ID:**
   - Online: https://www.mndor.state.mn.us/
   - Select "Register for a Tax ID"
   - Provide corporation information and EIN

2. **Sales Tax (if applicable):**
   - Determine if selling tangible goods in Minnesota
   - Digital products/software may be exempt
   - Cryptocurrency transactions generally not subject to sales tax

3. **Withholding Tax:**
   - Required if you will have employees
   - Register for withholding tax account

4. **Corporate Franchise Tax:**
   - Minnesota corporations pay 9.8% corporate income tax
   - File Form M4 (Minnesota Corporation Franchise Tax Return) annually
