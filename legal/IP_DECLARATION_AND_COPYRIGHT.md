## ROADCOIN: EXPLICIT NON-SECURITY CLASSIFICATION

### What RoadCoin IS:
- **Utility Token:** Used for governance and transaction fees within the RoadChain ecosystem
- **Decentralized Network Token:** Facilitates peer-to-peer operations on blockchain infrastructure
- **Open-Source Protocol:** Smart contract code publicly auditable and verifiable
- **Native Blockchain Asset:** Created and distributed on-chain with transparent supply cap
- **Non-Custodial:** Users maintain direct control of their tokens via cryptographic keys

### What RoadCoin IS NOT:
- ❌ **NOT a security** - No expectation of profit from the efforts of others (Howey Test)
- ❌ **NOT a derivative** - Not derived from or representing any underlying asset
- ❌ **NOT a debt instrument** - No promise of repayment, interest, or fixed returns
- ❌ **NOT an investment contract** - No pooling of funds, no management by third party
- ❌ **NOT a basket of financial products** - Single, standalone utility token
- ❌ **NOT leverage-based** - No margin, no borrowing, no leveraged exposure
- ❌ **NOT an Initial Public Offering (IPO)** - No sale of company equity to public investors
- ❌ **NOT a traditional fundraising mechanism** - Direct utility sale, not investment solicitation

### Regulatory Compliance Position:
RoadCoin is designed as a **functional utility token** similar to:
- Ethereum (ETH) - used for gas fees
- Filecoin (FIL) - used for decentralized storage
- Chainlink (LINK) - used for oracle services

**No Securities Registration Required** because RoadCoin:
1. Has intrinsic utility within the RoadChain network
2. Is sufficiently decentralized (no central controlling party)
3. Does not promise financial returns
4. Token economics are transparent and hardcoded in smart contract
5. Native blockchain verification eliminates custodial risk
