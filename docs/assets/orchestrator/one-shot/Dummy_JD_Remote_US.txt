Role: Senior Customer Success Manager (Remote - United States)
Company: Aurora Analytics

About the role:
Aurora Analytics enables mid-market retailers to forecast demand using
privacy-conscious data modeling. We are hiring a Senior Customer Success Manager
to own a portfolio of enterprise accounts and drive adoption of our predictive
insights platform.

Responsibilities:
- Lead quarterly business reviews and align product roadmaps to customer goals.
- Design adoption plans that expand use of AI forecasting modules.
- Coordinate with solutions engineering to resolve production issues.
- Analyze churn signals and proactively mitigate risk across your accounts.
- Own renewal and upsell motions alongside the sales organization.

Required experience:
- 5+ years in customer success or account management for B2B SaaS products.
- Proven track record managing $3M+ ARR portfolios with multi-threaded
  relationships.
- Expertise in retail, supply chain, or adjacent verticals.
- Ability to tell compelling stories using dashboards and executive-ready decks.
- Strong project management skills; PMP or similar certification is a plus.

Nice to have:
- Experience launching AI or machine-learning products with non-technical users.
- Familiarity with enterprise integration patterns (iPaaS, webhook pipelines).
- Background in change management or enablement programs.

To apply, share your resume and a short note describing how you grew adoption for
a complex platform.
