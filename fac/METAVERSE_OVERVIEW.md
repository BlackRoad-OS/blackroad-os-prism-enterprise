# Metaverse of Love and Light

## Our Vision

A peaceful, democratic metaverse built on love, consent, and community. A place where everyone has what they need, where voices matter equally, where families of all forms thrive, and where movement is free. No weapons. No violence. No kings, queens, or gods. Just us - supporting each other with words, care, and shared power from within.

## Core Principles

- **Love and Light**: The foundation of all we do
- **Consent**: Sacred and always required
- **Words**: We use our words to communicate and resolve
- **Popular Vote**: Democracy through direct participation
- **No Hierarchies**: No kings, no queens, no gods
- **Power Within**: From ourselves and each other
- **Resources for All**: Abundance shared freely
- **Respect**: For all living and non-living things

## Daily Life in the Metaverse

**Morning**: Wake in your personalized home, free and comfortable. Step through a portal to Tranquility Gardens for meditation. Meet friends at Heart Plaza for breakfast from the Resource Commons.

**Day**: Collaborate on art at Creation Studio. Learn something new at Wisdom Library. Vote on community proposals from anywhere. Help care for neighbor's children in Family Grove.

**Evening**: Gather at Celebration Circle for music and dancing. Share a feast with your chosen family. Express gratitude in community circle.

**Always**: Move freely. Access all resources. Participate in decisions. Connect with love. Resolve conflicts with words. Support each other.

## Getting Started

### For New Arrivals
1. Enter through Welcome Portal to Heart Plaza
2. Community greeters will welcome you
3. Receive orientation to spaces and systems
4. Set up your home in any location you choose
5. Access resources from Resource Commons
6. Explore spaces via Portal Hub
7. Participate in community votes
8. Connect with others at any gathering space

### For Families
1. Declare your family (any configuration)
2. Community celebrates with you
3. Access family-sized housing and resources
4. Connect with other families at Family Grove
5. Participate in parenting circles and support networks
6. Celebrate milestones together

### For Everyone
- **Resources**: Visit Resource Commons or request delivery
- **Transportation**: Use any portal, always free
- **Voting**: Access from Heart Plaza or online platform
- **Learning**: Wisdom Library and peer circles
- **Creating**: Creation Studio welcomes all
- **Peace**: Tranquility Gardens always open
